Check Availability & Make a Booking

Use the live Hallmaster booking system below to check availability and submit your booking request.

1

Check the calendar

Look through the live calendar below to find the date and room you would like to book.

2

Press the plus sign

Click the + button on your chosen date/time to start a new booking request.

3

Complete and submit

Fill in your details, event information and any room requirements, then submit the request for review.

Need help? If you cannot see the plus sign, try scrolling within the calendar area, changing the view, or opening the page on a larger screen. You can also contact HUDAWI Cultural Centre if you need support with your booking.

Bar and drinks

The venue features a fully stocked and licensed bar. If the hirer wishes to provide complimentary drinks for guests, they may do so by arranging a bar tab or by paying corkage per bottle. Alcohol cannot be sold independently by hirers unless this has been agreed in advance with the venue. Commercial hirers may bring their own alcohol stock for sale, provided they pay the commercial bar fee of £1,500 and can produce a valid Personal Licence.

For commercial bookings, use of the bar is charged at £1,500. For non-commercial bookings, the charge is £500.
